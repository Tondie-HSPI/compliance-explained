COMMERCIAL GENERAL LIABILITY POLICY

Coverage Summary
Commercial General Liability coverage is provided with limits of $1,000,000 each occurrence and $2,000,000 general aggregate.

Additional Insured Endorsement
Owner and contractor are included as additional insureds.

Waiver of Subrogation Endorsement
Waiver of subrogation applies in favor of owner and contractor.

Umbrella Liability Policy
Umbrella coverage is provided with a limit of $5,000,000.
