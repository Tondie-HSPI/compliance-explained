INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS

1. Commercial General Liability Insurance
The Contractor shall provide and maintain Commercial General Liability insurance with limits of not less than $1,000,000 each occurrence and $2,000,000 general aggregate. Coverage shall apply on an occurrence basis.

2. Additional Insured
The owner and contractor shall be included as additional insureds on the Commercial General Liability policy by endorsement.

3. Waiver of Subrogation
The Contractor shall provide a waiver of subrogation in favor of the owner and contractor where permitted by law.

4. Umbrella/Excess Liability
The Contractor shall provide Umbrella/Excess Liability coverage with a limit of not less than $5,000,000.
